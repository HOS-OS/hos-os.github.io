Chrome is a free Internet browser officially released by Google. Its features include synchronization with Google services and accounts, tabbed browsing, and automatic translation and spell check of web pages.

Chromium is a free and open-source web browser project, principally developed and maintained by Google.

Mozilla Firefox is a free open-source browser whose development is overseen by the Mozilla Corporation. Firefox gives users a cleaner interface and faster download speeds. Firefox also has a sync feature to see your open and recent tabs, browsing history, and bookmarks across all your devices.

The Tor browser works by using a technology known as onion routing. Onion routing uses multiple layers of encryption to conceal both the source and destination of information sent over the network.

Opera is a Web browser that provides some advantages over other browsers from Mozilla or Google. Opera is known for being fast and stable. Opera also has a built-in ad blocker which eliminates the need to use a third-party ad blocker.
